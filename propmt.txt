# ANONYMOUS PROTOCOL

## Generate ONLY the Opening Hero Experience (100vh)

You are **not designing a website**.

You are designing the **opening cinematic of a digital experience**.

This is the homepage for **Anonymous Club**, the Cybersecurity Club of SDMCET.

The goal is to create an unforgettable first impression that feels closer to an interactive movie than a landing page.

The first viewport should be the kind of experience people record and share because they've never seen a college website behave like this.

---

# IMPORTANT

Generate **ONLY the first viewport (100vh).**

Do not generate any sections below.

Do not create a normal homepage.

Do not create a hero layout.

Focus entirely on the opening experience.

---

# THE STORY

The visitor has just connected to Anonymous Headquarters.

The headquarters has been dormant.

It has been waiting.

The visitor's arrival activates the entire facility.

The website should tell this story without using paragraphs or explanations.

Everything should happen through visuals, motion and interaction.

---

# CINEMATIC SEQUENCE

## Phase 1

The page opens to almost complete darkness.

No visible interface.

No navigation.

No buttons.

No content.

Only silence.

A faint ambient glow barely illuminates the darkness.

Tiny distant particles drift slowly.

The visitor should wonder if the page has finished loading.

---

## Phase 2

A single pulse appears somewhere on the screen.

Not bright.

Just enough to catch attention.

The pulse expands across the darkness.

Like electricity travelling through invisible cables.

As it travels...

thin connection lines begin appearing.

Tiny nodes wake up.

One.

Then another.

Then another.

Nothing is random.

Everything feels deliberate.

---

## Phase 3

The signal continues travelling through the network.

Every node activates another node.

The visitor watches the headquarters literally boot itself.

The environment slowly becomes alive.

Data starts flowing.

Signals travel.

Connections pulse.

Tiny indicators wake up.

Very subtle.

Very premium.

Nothing flashy.

---

## Phase 4

The signal reaches the center.

Everything pauses.

The environment becomes completely still.

A tiny elegant system message appears.

ACCESS GRANTED

Immediately afterwards...

The headquarters quietly lights up.

The visitor realizes they are looking at an enormous digital operations center.

Not a website.

---

## Phase 5

Only now does the interface become interactive.

The visitor can move the mouse.

The cursor creates tiny signal ripples.

Nearby nodes react.

Connection lines brighten.

Small information labels appear.

Every movement makes the headquarters feel alive.
